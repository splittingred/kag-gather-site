Gather Rules

READ FIRST

  • Every player should have read KAG Gather Guide and Rules AT LEAST once.
  • This is a private project, meaning the owners can in theory do what they want.
  • We do this on our own spare time, we do not get paid. Instead, our work time is spent to provide you this service.
  • We are only human: we may make mistakes.
  • KAG Gather is and will be available for PREMIUM USERS only.

General

  • Respect other members, and especially the admins.
  • Do not whine or flame excessively.
  • Be nice and play fairly.
  • Use common sense.
  • Keep ALL IRC and in-game communication in English.

IRC Channel

  • Do not spam or flood the bot. Do not flood the IRC channel.
  • If you have added to a gather, do not leave the computer unattended. If the gather starts while you are away and you do not show up within 5 minutes, you will be punished.
  • The topic in #kag.gather may contain important information, read it every time you join.

Matches

  • Official KAG Gather rounds start always on map restart!
  • Hacking or attempts to hack as well as griefing intentional or not will result in a PERMA BAN. We share bans with all the other important places of KAG community.
  • A gather match is best of three rounds.
  • Spectators are allowed if the players in the gather are ok with it. Spectators are not to interfere in the game in any way.
  • Join the team for which you were assigned to by the Gather Bot. Switching teams is only allowed if ALL added players agree.
  • Ideally, your in-game nick should be the same as your IRC + forum nicks.
  • If an added player fails to show up to the server or leaves for no reason, get a sub.
  • Don't be lame. [This covers excessive whining, flaming, mocking, flag entombing etc.]
  • Bug abuse is not allowed and will be punished.
  • The best way to make sure someone is punished is a video/screenshots.
  • You are obligated to keep your ping at a minimum. Failing to do so will result in temporary ban.
  • You are obligated to play as best you can when playing KAG Gather! The idea of a gather is to play competitive matches in the style of a clanwar. Reserve your screwing around for public servers.
  • Do not leave the game until it is over.
  • No game modifications that give you an unfair advantage over the other players are allowed.
  • Everyone is able to restart map and nextmap. Don't abuse this command or you will be banned!
  • Flag entombing is prohibited.

Match Settings

  • Game type: 1 cap Premium CTF
  • Win condition: best of three (you need to win 2 of 3 matches)
  • Scale: 5 vs 5
  • Timer: 20 minutes
  • Maps: Arthurian League mapset generators (150x50; gold in the middle)
  • Starting resources: 250 Stone, 250 Wood
  • Teams: 1 Builder, 1 Archer, 3 Knights
  • Units: 25 Units per team = 5 units per person
  • Build time: 60 seconds
  • No switching between classes (Teams are to make up their own roles before each game.) (Although players are allowed to switch class ONLY after map but NOT during the match)
  • Glide on bomb jumps
  • Reduced wall damage on bomb jumps
  • Faster firing and gathering speed for archers
  • Bomb cost: 25c
  • Kegs available for gold and coins in siege shop
  • Premium lighting (darkness)
